ABOUT US

CLASSIC CARS GRANADA

An enthusiasts’ company!

Classic Cars has been set up by Alex and Rachel in order to enable as many car enthusiasts as possible to live the fantastic experience of driving a classic car in Granada.

They had two of them and one day some friends asked them to borrow one in order to give a “special touch” to their wedding anniversary... A few weeks later Classic Car was born!

Very quickly, they realized that the city was a tremendous “playground” for the classic cars they have a passion for, the small roads of the countryside as well as the drives on the seaside are a true delight behind the steering wheel...

Everybody fall in love with the region which is offering so many things to do and to see, troughout marvellous landscapes, between sea and mountains...
